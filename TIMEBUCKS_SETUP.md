# 📖 Timebucks AI Agent - Complete Setup Guide

## 📋 Overview

The Timebucks AI Agent automatically:
- ✅ Opens Timebucks surveys
- ✅ Detects survey questions
- ✅ Fills survey questions with AI-generated responses
- ✅ Handles multiple survey types (Toluna, Innovate, MXR, etc.)
- ✅ Tracks completion status
- ✅ Saves detailed results
